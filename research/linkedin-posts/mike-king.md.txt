## Post 1
Date: June 16, 2026

Measurement is the first thing generative AI broke, 
and most CMOs haven't admitted it yet.

For decades we lived and died by rankings and traffic. 
That worked when a user saw a list and picked a link. 
But AI Search doesn't return a list. The query fans out, 
the model synthesizes one answer, and ranking #1 stops 
mattering if you're not the citation the model trusts.

Classic search was a performance channel. AI Search 
behaves more like a brand channel, so you have to think 
about performance differently.

The mistake I see executives make: they watch classic 
rankings dip, assume the team is failing, and miss that 
brand influence is actually climbing. Wrong scoreboard.

Key Insight: The teams winning the next 24 months are 
rebuilding the scoreboard before the old one goes fully dark.
