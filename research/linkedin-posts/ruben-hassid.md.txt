Write the longest prompt you can. Here's how:

1. Open Claude. Don't type your first message yet.

✦ Claude re-reads your ENTIRE conversation on every message.

✦ Every reload is tokens. Tokens are money.

You're not lazy because you write short prompts. 
You write short prompts because typing is slow.
So stop typing.
